1. How to Delete Your Account/Data

We value your privacy. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Log in to your account: Visit our login page at http://localhost:3000/login and enter your credentials.
  2. Navigate to the Account Settings: Once logged in, click on the "Settings" or "Account" section found in your user dashboard.
  3. Select "Delete Account": Look for an option labeled "Delete Account" or "Remove my Data."
  4. Confirm the Deletion: You will be asked to confirm your decision. Please note that this action is irreversible.
  5. Follow the Confirmation Link: Check your email for a confirmation link. Click the link to finalize the deletion of your account and data.

2. What Happens After Deletion?

Once your deletion request is processed, your account will be permanently removed from our system, and your data will be erased according to our data retention policies.
